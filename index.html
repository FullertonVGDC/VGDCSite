Welcome to the VGDC

We are the Video Game Development Club! We are composed of students from California State University, Fullerton with an interest and passion in game development. We are an organization of students with the objective to make games, and our goal is to educate our members on all facets of game development.

Game Lab & General Meetings

Game Labs & General Meetings are held in CS 101 - Every Friday from 12 - 4 PM.
The computer lab is equipped with iMac desktops; however it is recommended to bring your own device.
Installed software currently includes the full Adobe Suite, Unity 5.6.0f3, git, and GitKraken.

Topics currently being discussed are finishing up our current game, Penguin Delivery System, before the end of the semester. The game project can be accesssed through our Github at FullertonVGDC.
