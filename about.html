About Us

The CSUF Video Game Development Club is a student-run organization under the college of Engineering & Computer Science. We are open to all majors and encourage our members to take up various disciplines as they work in teams to develop video games.

Our goal per semester is to design, develop, and complete at least one game project.
Our game projects can be found on our github here or our itch.io here.

Features

  • We all love video games! (And wanna make them!)
  • Workshops and resources to help beginners get started
  • Guest speakers, company tours, and industry insight
  • Game jams, socials, and other community events
