Game Labs

Weekly Game Labs for 2020 Spring Semester -

  • Every Friday from 12-4PM
  • Location: CS 300
  • We go over recent events related to the Game Development Industry, give time for teams to work on their semester projects,
    and host weekly workshops covering all aspects in Game Development. Find schedules for those below!
  • Note: Due to Coronavirus and health concerns, VGDC will be cancelling this Friday's Game Lab Meeting and hold a VIRTUAL
    meeting at 12PM this Friday (3/13) on Discord instead!
  • We will go over our usual announcements and showcase Slam Jam Games. Semester Project teams are expected to meet virtually
    with their team in place of the usual physical meeting at Game Lab
  • To repeat, do not go to CS 300 on 3/13 since meeting is purely on Discord. Contact us for any questions!

3D Modeling Workshop -

  • Date and Time: Pushed to Friday March 20th, 2020 2-4PM
  • Location: CSUF Campus CS 300
  • Learn the basics of using Autodesk Maya, an industry standrd 3D modeling nd rendering program to create
    3D props for 3D Games
  • The Workshop will also go over good practices when using Maya such as importing reference images, using layers,
    and learning other general tips so you can become a pro in Maya!
  • The workshop will have a hands-on portion teaching you how to model simple household objects. So please bring a
    a laptop with Autodesk Maya installed! (It is a hefty download, so do so ASAP!)

Club Events

Student Games Festival -

  • Date and Time: Sunday, April 19th, 2020 9am-6pm
  • Location: CSUF Campus Titan Student Union
  • This will be the greatest crossover event for Game Dev Clubs featuring two of the largest student game development organizations in the US.
  • SGDA will have a Student Expo, showcasing student games from other student Game Dev Clubs across Southern California!
  • IEEE GameSIG will present their 9th annual intercollegiate games showcase and competition
  • For students with a game project, this is the ultimate showcase for your hard work and a potentially great networking opportunity!
    We highly recommend you signing up to showcase your semester game project!
  • There will be free admission, free food, and free parking!
  • Stay tuned here or in any of our other social media platforms for more info!
