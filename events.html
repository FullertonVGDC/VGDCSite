Game Labs

Weekly Game Labs for 2020 Spring Semester -

  • Every Friday from 12-4PM
  • Location: CS 300
  • We go over recent events related to the Game Development Industry, give time for teams to work on their semester projects,
    and host weekly workshops covering all aspects in Game Development. Find schedules for those below!

Unreal Workshop -

  • Date and Time: Friday March 6th, 2020 2-4PM
  • Location: CSUF Campus CS 200
  • Want to take the first step in programming Video Games in Unreal? Come to our Intro to Unreal Blueprint Workshop!
  • We will be covering the basics 3D Game Development using Blueprints led by Vijay Duggirala!
  • The entire workshop will be hands-on so please install a version of the Unreal Engine if you wish to participate!
    Unreal is a hefty download so please do it ASAP!
  • Time permitted, we will also covering basics of version control with Unreal Projects
  • Notes will be presented afterwards for Paid Members!

Club Events

G&E Indie Game Night -

  • Date and Time: Friday March 6th, 2020 4-8PM
  • Location: CSUF Campus TSU Legislative Chambers
  • We will be attending Gaming & E-Sport’s Indie Game Night right after our Game Lab!
  • You will be able to showcase your game projects there - as long as you bring your own equipment
    This is a good chance to get feedback and QA testing!
  • If you wish to showcase a game, please fill out this form by Tuesday, March 3rd, 2020 at 12:00PM!

VGDC 24 Hour Game Jam: Slam Jam! -

  • Date and Time: Saturday and Sunday, March 7th and 8th, 2020 10am-11pm
  • Location: CSUF Campus CS200 and CS202
  • Everybody get up, it’s time to jam now! We got a real jam goin’ down! Welcome to the Slam Jam!
    Here’s your chance, do your dance at the Slam Jam! Alright...
  • Come to our last game jam of the 2019-2020 School Year! Find a team and create a game within 24 hours
    separated in two 12 hour days
  • Check in will begin in CS 200 at 10AM on March 7 (Saturday)
  • We will give you one free meal day and supply free snacks and water!
  • Parking is free on campus on the weekends! Closest spaces are in Lot E or Eastside Structure
  • Please be prepared and bring your own equipment (laptops, chargers, etc) to participate!
  • Please RSVP Here: http://www.tinyurl.com/VGDCSlamJam
