Game Labs

Weekly Game Labs for 2020 Spring Semester -

  • Every Friday from 12-4PM
  • Location: CS 300
  • We go over recent events related to the Game Development Industry, give time for teams to work on their semester projects,
    and host weekly workshops covering all aspects in Game Development. Find schedules for those below!
  • Note: We will not have a game lab on February 21st, 2020 in observance of President's Day!

Pre-Production Workshop - How to Get Started on Your Game!

  • Date and Time: Friday February 14th, 2020 1:30 - 3:30pm
  • Location: CSUF Campus CS 300
  • Lead by a variety of our own Executive Board and General Officers
  • Learn how to start your developing your game off strong!
  • We will be going over how to create an effective schedule, delegate tasks, and early game design!
  • For Team Leads and new members to the club, this is highly recommended so you can better understand the Game Project process in the club!
