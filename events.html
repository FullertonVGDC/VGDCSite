Lucky Charm Game Jam

  • We are partnering with CSUF Polysquad and together we will be hosting our first Game Jam of the semester on March 13th
  • Form teams and create new games in under 24 hours.

    • Speakers

  • On March 20th at 2pm we will have TWO speakers in one night!
  • We will have Loren Colcol and Oliver Wang

    • Loren is a game producer at PlayStation, San Diego Studio. He started his career at Pixar Animation Studios, providing technical support to artists, animators, and storytellers before transitioning into AAA game development. In the last few years at PlayStation, he has worked on development teams to ship yearly titles of MLB The Show, as well as incubate R&D teams exploring new IP at Sony.

    Oliver is a growth marketer specializing in mobile gaming. He is currently leading marketing at Cinamon Games, a small studio making interactive fiction games. Previously he worked at Elevate, a brain-training games developer, and Nexon, a PC/mobile games publisher. Some mobile IP he's worked on include Titanfall, Marvel, Dynasty Warriors, and Maplestory.
