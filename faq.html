Questions Frequently Asked About our Organization

What is our goal this year?
This academic year our goal is to, Make a Game. That simple the majority of our members have yet to finish a game, and this year all of our members will be able to claim that they created something Awesome.
I want to participate, but I don't program and cannot draw. What can I do?
In game development there are many different roles one can undertake as a developer. Programers and artists make up the majority of our mmembership base, but there are many other roles. For example, if game designers, also called level designers conceptualize what can be found in a level, create different ideas for character and objects, and give an overall vision for a game. In addition there are music designers. Music designers use music to create a general mood for a game. There are other roles as well. Here at VGDC we want every one of our members to find there place in video game development.
